Terms

These terms explain what you are (and aren’t) buying. Short version: it’s a novelty certificate referencing a randomly generated location tile.

1) What you’re buying

You are buying a novelty digital certificate that references a randomly generated area tile (for example, 1 m × 1 m) identified by geographic coordinates.

You are not buying land. You receive no property rights, ownership, lease, easement, tenancy, or any other legal interest in any real-world land or location.

2) Access and permissions

The referenced tile may be located on public land, private property, restricted areas, culturally sensitive sites, hazardous terrain, construction zones, closed tracks, or otherwise inaccessible places.

We do not grant access. If you choose to visit, you must follow local laws, rules, signage, closures, and obtain permission where required. Do not trespass.

3) “Publicish” mode is not a guarantee

Some tiles are generated using a best-effort “publicish” filter. This tries to avoid obvious ocean/remote picks using public map data. This is not a guarantee that the tile is accessible, legal to visit, safe, open, or even on land.

4) Randomness + rerolls

Tiles are randomly generated. You can reroll before purchase, but you do not get to choose an exact location on a map. Once you pay, the purchase corresponds to the tile stored with that checkout session.

5) Deliverable

After payment is verified, you can download your certificate as an image file. The certificate includes the tile’s center coordinates, tile size, and seed identifier.

6) Refunds

Because this is a digital novelty item delivered immediately after payment verification, refunds are generally not provided. If something is broken (e.g., you paid but cannot download), contact support and we’ll sort it out.

7) Safety

Don’t do anything dumb for the bit. If you visit a tile, use common sense and prioritize safety. Avoid dangerous conditions, respect private property, and check weather, track closures, and access rules.

Support email: randomspotcert@gmail.com
